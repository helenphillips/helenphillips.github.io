About Me

I'm a 3rd year PhD student from Imperial College London, although based at the Natural History Museum, London. I work within the PREDICTS project, with my supervisor Andy Purvis.

The main focus of my research is investigating the effects of land use change and habitat fragmentation on biodiversity. This involves collating data from researches across the globe, and combining it with freely available remotely-sensed data, such as MODIS.

I am a developer and maintainer of the R package MODISTools, with Sean Tuck.
